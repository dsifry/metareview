# metareview Learning Review Rubric

Post-merge learning keeps only knowledge that changes future reviewer behavior.

## Accept

- The candidate describes a reusable review behavior, not a one-off fact.
- The candidate has provenance and enough confidence to act on later.
- The candidate is not already represented in Beads or fallback metareview knowledge.
- The candidate belongs in repository review knowledge or reviewer calibration.

## Discard

Use exactly one compact reason:

- `too-specific`
- `self-evident`
- `already-covered`
- `obsolete`
- `follow-up-not-knowledge`
- `registry-update-not-knowledge`
- `unverified`

Do not keep detail merely because it is specific. Prefer a discard log entry over durable knowledge when the lesson would not alter a future review.
